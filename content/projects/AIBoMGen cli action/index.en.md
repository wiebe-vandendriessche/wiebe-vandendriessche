GitHub Action wrapper around the AIBoMGen CLI for CI/CD pipelines. Enables automated generation, validation, enrichment, and merging of [CycloneDX](https://cyclonedx.org/) AIBOMs during builds. Created in the context of the [CRACY Project](https://cra-cy.eu/) to make AI/ML supply chain transparency accessible to SMEs without requiring manual tooling.
