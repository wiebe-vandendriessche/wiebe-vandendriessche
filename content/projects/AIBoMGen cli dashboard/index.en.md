Web-based demo dashboard built on top of the AIBoMGen CLI, combining a Go backend and a [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) frontend. Provides an interactive interface to generate, validate, and inspect [CycloneDX](https://cyclonedx.org/) AI Bills of Materials. Designed as a [CRACY Project](https://cra-cy.eu/) showcase for making AI/ML [SBOM](https://www.ntia.gov/sbom) generation accessible through a user-friendly UI.
